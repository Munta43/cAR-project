cAR User Documentation

Getting Started

  1. Install cAR from the Apple App Store.
  2. Launch the app and wait for the home page to appear.
  3. If you swipe left on the image, you can see more images and descriptions of what cAR can do.
    1. Swipe right to see a previous image.

Sign Up

  1. Press the ‘Sign Up’ button on the home page.
  2. Choose to sign in with an existing account, sign up with our app, or sign up with Facebook, Google, or Apple ID.
    1. Sign in
      1. Press the grey ‘Sign in’ text at the bottom of the screen.
      2. Please refer to the ‘Sign In’ section
    2. Sign up with our app
      1. Enter your full name, email, and your password in the appropriate fields.
        1. Press ‘Sign Up’ button
    3. Sign up with Facebook, Google, or Apple ID
      1. Press the button for the service you wish to sign up with
      2. You will be taken to a webpage of the service you selected and they will guide you through the sign up process.
